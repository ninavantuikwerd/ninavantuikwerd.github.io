Nina van Tuikwerd

Stranger in Paradise

mixed media


In the documentary Stranger in Paradise, a teacher subjects refugees in a classroom to the many faces of Europe. He approaches them first cold and harsh, then sympathetic and patronizing, followed by a business approach according to the asylum rules. The different states: dreams, rules and reality were visualized by three interventions in school. Followed by a publication with newsarticles about the three different views. Three digital printed posters on fabric were made from the images in the newsarticles.
