Nina van Tuikwerd

Stranger in Paradise


Based on the documentary Stranger in Paradise; A teacher subjects refugees in a classroom to the many faces of Europe. He approaches them first cold and harsh, then sympathetic and patronizing, followed by a business approach according to the asylum rules. With these three states, Rules, Dream and the Reality I made three interventions in school. Followed by a publication with newsarticles about the three different views. From the images in the newsarticles I made digital printed posters on fabric.
